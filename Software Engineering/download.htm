Transform Your Canteen with our Canteen Management System

Every organization or institution has its canteen, be it schools, colleges, hospitals, MNCs; canteen management might take a lot of time if done manually. Hence, a canteen management system helps manage attendance, food consumption, and menu with ease.
The Canteen Management System is an easy way to manage the activities of the canteen with the help of user-friendly automated software that maintains a detailed account of the food served at the canteen. It provides real-time user detail and comes with a customizable menu fast. It enables cashless, secure, and speedy transactions.
The canteen management system manages every aspect of the canteen and is integrated with the back office. It is backed up by credential support such as Face Recognition, Bluetooth, and Fingerprint Unlock option to ensure safety. The system is well integrated with any standard printer and helps to print receipts and transaction details. It allows the generation of cafeteria reports which can be exported in any format. Along with real-time food consumption details and tracking of food expenses, it allows the request for correction of erroneous entry.
